Sourdough Bread

While sourdough bread may seem difficult to make at first, it is something anyone can do!

All you'll need is:

  • Water
  • Flower
  • Salt
  • An Oven
  • A baking sheet or iron skillet
  • A timer

Getting Started

the first step is to create a starter

Here is great video for making your first sourdough starter

Mixing the dough

  1. combine 3 parts flower to 1 part water
  2. mix together flower and water until a stick blob
  3. add starter and a pinch of salt

Rising

  • After forming dough into balls leave covered for 2-12 hours

Score and Bake!

drag a razor across the top of your risen dough then Bake at 500-600 degrees until golden
